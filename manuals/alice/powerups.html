P O W E R U P S


Powerups only work for a limited time. Fortunately, you’ll find them strategically placed throughout Wonderland.

Ragebox – Increases Alice’s strength and conserves her Will. The Ragebox rocks.


Grasshopper Tea – A sip is guaran-tea’d to improve Alice’s speed and agility.

Darkened Looking Glass – Use it to creep unseen through creepy places.

Deadtime Watch [Default Key 0] – Everything but Alice stands still when she uses the Watch!
